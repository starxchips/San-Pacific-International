Questions

San Pacific International FAQ

Everything you need to know about our sculptures, materials, care, and where to find them.

  • What materials are SPI sculptures made from?

    San Pacific International sculptures are crafted using high-quality materials such as brass, aluminum, cast iron, and resin, ensuring both beauty and durability for indoor and outdoor use.

  • Are the sculptures suitable for outdoor display?

    Yes, many SPI sculptures feature weather-resistant finishes that protect them from the elements, making them ideal for gardens, patios, and other outdoor spaces.

  • How are the finishes applied?

    Each sculpture receives hand-applied patina finishes using a proprietary process that involves blow torches and chemical treatments, creating unique, layered effects on every piece.

  • Can I find SPI sculptures in retail stores?

    Yes, San Pacific International products are often available through specialty décor retailers and collectible galleries such as Fine Decor Direct and Biggs Ltd.

  • Are SPI sculptures considered collectible art?

    Absolutely. Due to their craftsmanship, unique finishes, and limited production, many SPI sculptures are highly valued by collectors and interior designers alike.

  • How do I care for and maintain my SPI sculpture?

    Regular dusting and gentle cleaning with a soft cloth will help maintain the sculpture's appearance. For outdoor pieces, occasional checks for patina wear and protection from extreme weather are recommended.

  • Can I order custom or commissioned pieces?

    While SPI mainly produces established designs, some retailers or galleries may assist with special requests or limited editions — it's best to contact authorized dealers for details.

  • What is the typical size range of SPI sculptures?

    SPI offers a wide variety of sizes, from small indoor gallery pieces to larger garden statues and functional art like benches and fountains. Specific dimensions vary by model.
